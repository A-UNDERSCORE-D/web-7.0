[Scheduled Maintenance] Switch Maintenance

 christel on 2004-12-29

Good morn­ing and best wish­es dur­ing the hol­i­day sea­son. One of our spon­sors has sched­uled switch main­te­nance for New Years' Eve morn­ing, 31 De­cem­ber 2004 at 1am UTC.
