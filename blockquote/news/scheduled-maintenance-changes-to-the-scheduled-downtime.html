[Scheduled Maintenance] Changes to the scheduled downtime

 christel on 2005-06-28

There's a slight change in the outage schedule. The outages are now planned to last up to an hour and a half, beginning at 8:30am UTC (1:30am Los Angeles, 10:30am Brussels and 5:30pm Sydney) on: Wednesday, 29 June 2005 Sunday, 3 July 2005

We'll try to keep things running as well as possible during the outage periods, but there will be disruption--we appreciate your understanding and patience.
