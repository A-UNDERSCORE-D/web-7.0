Connecting with SASL

SASL is a method that al­lows iden­ti­fi­ca­tion to ser­vices (Nick­Serv) dur­ing the con­nec­tion process, be­fore any­thing else hap­pens - there­fore elim­i­nat­ing the need to /msg nick­serv iden­ti­fy. To use SASL, you must reg­is­ter your nick­name.

SASL Client Configuration
