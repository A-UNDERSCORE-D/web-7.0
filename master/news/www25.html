Happy Birthday to the World Wide Web

 Swant on 2016-08-06

25 years ago - On the 6'th of Au­gust 1991 - the world's first web­site went live, and it's liv­ing still. (proof)

In oth­er words the world wide web is cel­e­brat­ing sil­ver ju­bilee to­day.

Imag­ine were we would've been with­out it. Would your project ex­ist? Would freen­ode ex­ist?

On be­half of freen­ode, I'd like to give all our spon­sors a big shoutout, for mak­ing freen­ode pos­si­ble, read more about who they are on our ac­knowl­edge­ments page.

And last­ly We would like to thank YOU, the peo­ple that vis­it freen­ode, the peo­ple who use freen­ode for your projects, the peo­ple that con­tribute to keep­ing the in­ter­net full of Free Open Source Soft­ware and Peer Di­rect­ed Projects. You are a part of what took the in­ter­net this far and a part of what keep it, and a lot of oth­er tech re­lat­ed things, evolv­ing.

Stay with us, it's freen­ode's porce­lain ju­bilee this year, keep chat­ting, keep the in­ter­net open.
