[Scheduled Maintenance] Server Upgrades

 christel on 2005-06-20

freen­ode has ten­ta­tive­ly sched­uled serv­er up­grades for 6am UTC on Sat­ur­day 2 July 2005 (2am in New York City, 8am in Brus­sels, 4pm in Syd­ney and 11pm Fri­day in Los An­ge­les).
