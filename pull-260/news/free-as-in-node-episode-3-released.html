Free-as-in-node episode 3 released!

 JonathanD on 2009-04-13

The third in­stall­ment of  free-as-in-n­ode has just been re­leased! Head on over to http://pod­cast.freen­ode.net to lis­ten or down­load.

In this episode, you'll hear all about freen­odes april fools joke and our ex­plod­ing growth (cur­rent­ly at around 57,000 on­line users).  Also dis­cussed is the php test­fest with in­put from mgdm (Michael Maclean) in this weeks pimp my project, and then a bit of soap­box­ing and the an­nounce­ment of the new "freen­ode par­ty" in UK pol­i­tics, so that we too can par­take of free-as-in-shoes-y­our-­tax­pay­er­s-bought-y­ou.  Make sure you sign our pe­ti­tion be­fore Chris­tel shuts down freen­ode!

In ad­di­tion, we'll let you know whats go­ing on in the world with our take on the talks be­tween Sun and IBM, De­bian eat­ing FreeB­SD, the new Gnome 3.0 roadmap, and news on Tom­Tom's set­tle­ment with Mi­crosoft.  We'll also talk about Fe­do­ra11­be­ta with up­dates on our ex­pe­ri­ence on the next episode.

We also dis­cuss the "geeknic" once again with the an­nounce­ment of yet an­oth­er geeknic, this one in the Philadel­phia area in less than a week! If you can make it, bring us a ca­noe or kayak.

This week clos­es again with a won­der­ful morsel from Bryn_S.  We'll see you in two weeks!
